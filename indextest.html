Mental Health and Social Media

Introduction

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), half of all mental health conditions start in adolescence, yet most remain undetected or untreated. Depression is the fourth leading cause of illness and disability among adolescents. Social media usage patterns can have profound effects on mental health, influencing depression, anxiety, and sleep quality.

About the Datasets

Dataset 1: Social Media and Mental Health (SMMH)

This dataset explores the relationship between social media usage and mental health. It includes attributes like Age, Gender, Time Spent on Social Media, and various mental health indicators.

Data Access: Kaggle

Dataset 2: MMC2

This dataset examines the use of social networking sites and mental health issues among young adults in Bangladesh. It includes data on demographics, social media usage, and psychometric assessments.

Data Access: PMC Article
